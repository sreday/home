Ian Eyberg

Ian Eyberg

NanoVMs

Unikernels have been running in production for years now and that experience has produced a set of new tools, best practices and workflows from that experience.

In this talk come hear about how and why your peers have been pushing unikernels to prod and how new methods have been established. Whether it's cloud specific monitoring, accessing GPU resources for ML workloads or applying security policies that Linux workloads can't we'll show you how companies, large and small, have been heavily investing in unikernel deployment.
